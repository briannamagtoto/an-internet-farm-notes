The inspiration: 
![je-newspaper-inspo|300](https://hc-cdn.hel1.your-objectstorage.com/s/v3/0e075d4804a5095b9c19a9724329e483836e0735_img_2102.png)
& recently, I've been thinking about community building. I think physical mediums of connection are increasingly becoming rare, and for adequate reason, but I want to bring back the handcrafted and intimate nature of the newspaper.

I envision people delivering these newspapers at different entryways, and even to first-years on Old Campus. 

I was not very involved in JE last year but now I have great pride in being part of the community. My goal is to connect with people and to help people connect with each other. Also, I'm a bit of a nostalgic person so having a tangible representation of the year would bring me great joy. 

My vision is that it'll operate like a silly newspaper, with different columns and pieces of content that are relatively JE specific. 

This will largely be a self-directed project.

Current visions for content:
Surveys & make posters that advertise surveys in wacky ways - put 3D printed figures with QR codes in fun places. Googly eyes. 

Place where people can stick photos of themselves
-> Photo collage of people in JE 

JE postcards (The JE Express)
